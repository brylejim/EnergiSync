How to create an account?

Users cannot create their own accounts directly. Account creation is solely managed by the admin or property manager. If you need an account or have issues with account setup, please contact your respective admin for assistance.

How do I change my profile details?

Your profile details such as your name, customer account number, electric submeter number, and address are fixed and cannot be modified or updated. These details are set by the admin and are used for identification and management purposes.

How do I change my password?

To change your password, navigate to the Account and Settings section and find Security. Here, you can update your password. It is recommended to change your password right away once your login details was given by the admin. Create a password that is at least 6 characters long and includes a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. This helps ensure your account remains secure.

Why my total consumption does not update everytime I refresh the page?

If your total consumption does not update immediately when you refresh the page, there might be temporary server issues. While these issues are being resolved, rest assured that your consumption is still being accurately recorded. The data will appear correctly on your interface once the issue is fixed.

Is the bill amount displayed is exactly how much I should pay for?

The bill amount displayed on the website is mere approximation, as electricity rates and billing structures can vary over time. The displayed amount may not match your official billing statement and for bill prediction only, so please refer to your official bill for the precise amount due.

How do I retrieve my past consumption that is more than 2 years ago?

Consumption data older than 2 years ago is not accessible through the system. The system retains only the consumption records from the past 2 years ago for viewing. For records beyond this period, you may need to check with your utility provider for any possible archived data.
