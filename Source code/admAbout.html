DESCRIPTION

Monitor your apartment's electricity consumption in this website, designed to streamline your management and services for tenants.

The EnergiSync portal keeps your apartment's necessary consumption information in this free and user-friendly website. With the EnergiSync portal, you can:

  • Track total users
  • Generate account for your tenant
  • Receive outage alerts
  • Get the latest news and updates communicated by the electricity network
