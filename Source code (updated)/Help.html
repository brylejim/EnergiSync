HELP

How do I change my profile details?

Your profile details, such as your account number, cannot be modified or updated. These details are fixed and is used for identification purposes.

How do I change my password?

To change your password, navigate to the Account and Settings section and find Security. Here, you can update your password. Create a password that is at least 6 characters long and includes a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. This helps ensure your account remains secure.

Why my total consumption does not update everytime I refresh the page?

If your total consumption does not update immediately when you refresh the page, there might be a temporary server issues. While these issues are being resolved, rest assured that your consumption is still being accurately recorded. The data will appear correctly on your interface once the issue is fixed.

Is the bill amount displayed is exactly how much I should pay for?

The bill amount displayed on the website is mere approximation, as electricity rates and billing structures can vary over time. The displayed amount may not match your official billing statement and for bill prediction only, so please refer to your official bill for the precise amount due.
