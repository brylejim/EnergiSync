ABOUT

DESCRIPTION

View your electricity usage, approximated bill amount, and more in this website tailored for households.

The EnergiSync portal keeps your necessary consumption information in this free and user-friendly website. With the EnergiSync portal, you can:

  • Monitor your real-time electricity consumption just in one click
  • View your predicted consumption cost
  • Know your day-to-day usage patterns
  • Receive a monthly summary of your consumption
  • Know the available mediums for payment transactions
  • Get the latest news and updates communicated by the electricity network
  • Gather tips on saving energy
